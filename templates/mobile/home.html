THE GREENBLOX MISSION

Greenblox uses IoT devices to collect real-time pollution data from Smart Cities in various countries. After measuring this data against the goals these countries pledged at the Paris Climate Agreement, Greenblox uses a blockchain architecture to reward the countries that best meet their stated goals with "carbon tokens." These tokens can then be converted into tangible terms, such as World Bank loans and UN influence.

THE GREENBLOX TEAM

We are a group of tech innovators based in New York City and Silicon Valley, who hope to harness the power of IoT, blockchain, and AI to aid the global environmental movement.
